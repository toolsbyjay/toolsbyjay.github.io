About US

This organization/NGO was started by the 12th science students in back 2014.

The young members of NGO are working in the direction to provide education and employment to poor people and uplift their standard of living simultaneously.

With the help of all members of NGO, we got the permission by charity commissioner of Ahmedabad to work all over India.

Our organization is engaged in social works such as food packet distribution, stationary distribution, PASTI TO PATHSHALA, blood donation, Education, and women empowerment.

MISSION.
1) Provide education to poor people
2) To start a project of providing employment to poor people.
3) Work in the field of education and health.
4) work for woman empowerment

In future our NGO will try to run the CSR projects of big companies and projects of government underwhich we can increase the literacy rate of India.

We will try to provide education and employment to poor people so that they also get equal status in society.

Unity of Youngistan (meaning different identity of Young-Hindustan in Hindi / Gujarati) Our Youth Soldier hopes to create a new India.

This institute was started in 2012 by 12-Science students of Devasya International Public School.

The expectation of the youth soldiers of the organization is to provide education and employment to the lower class people and to make efforts to bring their lives above the poverty line.

With the help of all the members, we had to get the registration certificate from the Charity Commissioner, Ahmedabad in 2017 so that the organization can work all over India.

Food packets, stationery distribution, Pasti Se Pathshala, Blood Donation, Education, and Women’s Development will be done by our organization.

Approach

In future our organization will run projects for the poor on csr project and government scheme of big company of India and make efforts towards increasing education rate in India.

Efforts should be made to provide education and employment to women, children, youth in the poverty stricken areas so that the people in the lower classes maintain harmony in the society.
